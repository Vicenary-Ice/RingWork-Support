Ring Work is an AI shadow boxing trainer app that uses your phone's camera to track punches and provide real-time coaching feedback. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Camera & Body Tracking Data

Ring Work uses your device's camera for real-time body tracking. Your camera feed is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. No video, images, or raw camera data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted off your device. Pose landmark data is computed locally and discarded when your session ends. Ring Work does not have access to any footage of you.

2. Session & Training Data

Ring Work logs your training sessions locally on your device, including: session date and duration, round configuration, punch counts by type, drill accuracy scores, and weekly training volume. This data powers your in-app progress charts and is not linked to any account or personal identifier.

3. No Account Required

Ring Work does not require an account. No personal information (name, email, date of birth) is needed to use the app. If you purchase a premium subscription, billing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store — Ring Work never receives your payment details.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. Ring Work operates no servers or cloud storage. Deleting the app permanently removes all stored data. iCloud Backup may back up your local app data depending on your iOS settings.

5. Analytics & Crash Reporting

Ring Work may use Apple's anonymized analytics tools if you have opted in through iOS settings. This data contains no camera data or training logs and is used only to fix bugs. You can control this under Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

6. Third-Party Services

Ring Work does not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms. Ring Work uses RevenueCat to manage premium subscriptions and verify purchase status. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase and subscription information (such as a random app-generated identifier and transaction details) to deliver this functionality; it does not receive your name, payment card details, or training data. Payments themselves are handled by Apple through the App Store. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy for more information.

7. Children's Privacy

Ring Work is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Choices & Controls

You can revoke camera access at any time under Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera. Session logs can be cleared from within the app. Deleting the app removes all local data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Ring Work after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app isn't detecting my punches

Check your camera framing, stand 6–8 feet back from your device, and make sure you're in a well-lit area.

How do I change my stance?

You can change your stance in the app settings before starting a session.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to cancel.

How do I delete my training data?

You can clear your training data from within the app settings.
